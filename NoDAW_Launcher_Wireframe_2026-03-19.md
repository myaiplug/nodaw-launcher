## 2. Animated Onboarding & Progressive Reveal
- On first launch, logo animates in, features "boot up" in sequence.
- As features are unlocked, new visual effects and backgrounds are revealed.
- Achievements and easter eggs animate into the UI as users explore.

---

## 3. Unlock Animation & Feature Gating UX
- Locked tiles are greyed with a glowing lock icon.
- On click, a modal animates in: "Enter License Key or Upgrade Now".
- On unlock, tile animates: lock shatters, color blooms, tile pulses.
- Upsell copy and tooltips highlight benefits of each locked feature.
